Common questions about our SEO service

What does entity-based SEO mean in practice?

It means structuring your site so that search engines and AI systems can confidently describe what your business is, what it does, where it operates, and who it serves — not just match keyword strings.

How is this different from keyword stuffing or buying backlinks?

Those tactics create shallow signals that fade or fail audits. Entity-based SEO builds deep relevance through structure, clarity, schema, and consistent signals across your entire digital presence.

How long before we see results in Cambodia?

Technical improvements show crawl and indexation gains within weeks. Meaningful ranking and lead quality improvement typically requires consistent iteration over three to six months.

What do you need from us to get started?

Analytics and Search Console access, your target services and locations, and examples of the enquiry types you want to attract. We build the roadmap from there.

Do you support Khmer and English content?

Yes. In Cambodia, buyers often search in both languages before contacting a business. We support multilingual intent mapping and content strategy for both audiences.
