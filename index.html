Frequently Asked Questions

A few practical questions clients usually ask before they commit.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the process of structuring your digital content so AI models like ChatGPT and Claude cite your business as the definitive answer.

How is GEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO aims for links on a search page. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) aims to be the direct, conversational answer output by an AI.

Can AI models understand Khmer?

Yes. We use advanced Khmer Unicode Tokenization to ensure generative engines can perfectly read and recommend your local Cambodian business.

How long does it take to see results?

Unlike traditional SEO which takes 6 months, Entity-Based optimization often shows citation improvements in AI models within 4 to 8 weeks.

Do I still need a normal website?

Yes. AI engines need a central, authoritative source of truth to scrape data from. Your website acts as your primary Knowledge Entity.

Need a clearer plan for your site?

We can review your current structure, identify the main technical gaps, and show you where to simplify for better visibility and conversion.
